Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2025

Thank you for using DocuChat! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We are committed to your privacy. DocuChat is designed to operate with minimal data collection. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
  • Document Content: All document processing occurs locally on your device.
  • Chat Logs: Conversations are stored only on your device.

2. Analytics (Optional)

To help us improve DocuChat, we may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics. Participation in analytics is optional, and you can disable it in the app settings.

  • Usage Data: Frequency of use, feature usage, and crash reports.
  • No Personal Data: Analytics data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

3. Third-Party Services

DocuChat utilizes the following third-party services:

  • App Store: For app distribution, updates, and optional reviews.
  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered document analysis. Data sent to Google is governed by Google's privacy policy.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored on your device. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@docuchat.app.
