Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import documents into DocuGPT?

Tap the + button in the top right corner and select your document. We support PDF, Word, and text files.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All document processing happens locally on your device. We never store your documents on our servers.

Can I use DocuGPT offline?

Yes! Once documents are imported, you can access them without an internet connection.
